DoodleBoard

A collaborative drawing program, that performs like an app, but in a web browser.

DoodleBoard At School

One use case for DoodleBoard is in a classroom environment, as a means of engaging students to participate in a lesson. Art classes would especially benefit from this as its artistic possibilities are obvious and quite endless. Mathematics classes could allow students to answer simple math problems on their own devices, or collaboratively work on a single problem.

DoodleBoard At Work

When used in a business setting it can be used to increase the efficiency of meetings by allowing participants to offer their own input, sketch out an idea or a concept, or even in helping to keep all members interested and paying attention. DoodleBoard makes it easy to offer input quickly and efficiently.

DoodleBoard At Home

DoodleBoard can also be used in a home to allow you and your friends to draw all at the same time for fun. It can be used as a game board for Pictionary, or to doodle on family pictures or home movies. The things that can be done on DoodleBoard are limited only by the creativity of the users, in turn allowing for an endless amount of fun or productivity.
